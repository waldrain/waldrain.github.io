The COP values listed below are often too small, since hot water was being used while the heat pump was working, replaced by fresh cold water. They are calculated using the formula

cop = f * (Te - Tb) / C

with

C = consumption in kWh
Tb = temperature begin in K
Te = temperature end in K
f = 0.3382066667 = (4184 * 291) / (3600 * 1000)

where

c = 4184 J/(kg k) specific heat capacity of water
mass = 291 kg for 291 litres of water
1 kWh = 3600 * 1000 Joule
